## 📋 Project Overview
 
<div align="center">

<br/>
> **The financial services industry faces a mounting challenge: traditional loan recovery methods are failing at scale.** Industry data confirms that conventional human-agent-driven recovery methods yield success rates **below 20%**, while the global debt collection software market — valued at **USD 4.92 billion in 2023** — grows at a CAGR of approximately **9.6%**, signalling urgent demand for smarter alternatives.
 
This research proposes and examines an **AI-Based Smart Loan Recovery Calling System** — an integrated framework that leverages:
 
- 🗣️ **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** — Understanding borrower intent across 10+ languages and 500+ dialects
- 🎙️ **Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) & Text-to-Speech (TTS)** — Real-time voice processing
- 🧠 **Sentiment Analysis (BERT/LLM)** — Detecting distress, anger, and cooperation in real time
- 📊 **Predictive Analytics (XGBoost, LightGBM)** — Repayment Propensity Scoring per borrower
- ⚖️ **RBI Compliance Engine** — Contact windows, disclosure mandates, audit logging enforced by design
- 🤖 **Machine Learning Pipeline** — Continuous model improvement with chronological train-test splits

## 🧪 AI Call Flow
 
```
📞 CALL INITIATED
      │
      ▼
  STEP 1 ──► 🆔 Identification & Disclosure
             AI identifies itself + lending institution (RBI mandate)
      │
      ▼
  STEP 2 ──► 🔍 Intent Probing
             Bot inquires about overdue awareness + payment ability
      │
      ▼
  STEP 3 ──► 💰 Offer Presentation
             Personalised repayment options (based on RPS + segment)
      │
      ▼
  STEP 4 ──► ✅ Payment Facilitation
             UPI link via SMS/WhatsApp | Promise to Pay recorded
      │
      ▼
  STEP 5 ──► 🚨 Escalation (if needed)
             Disputes / distress → Human Agent + full context summary
```

## 📈 Real-World Case Studies
 
### 🇮🇳 Case Study 1 — Rezo AI (India, Leading NBFC)
 
| Metric | Before AI | After AI | Change |
|:-------|:---------:|:--------:|:------:|
| Collection Efficiency | 85% | **97%** | 🟢 +12 pts |
| Customer Satisfaction | 62% | **89%** | 🟢 +27 pts |
| Human Escalation Rate | 100% | **22%** | 🟢 -78 pts |
| Deployment Time | — | **2 weeks** | ⚡ Go-live |
| Languages Supported | — | **10+ / 500+ dialects** | 🌐 |
 
> **Key Lesson:** Linguistic localisation is essential. AI simultaneously improves recovery rates AND borrower satisfaction — disproving the assumption these goals are in tension.
 
---
 
### 🇲🇽 Case Study 2 — Apifonica (Mexico, Microloans)
 
| Metric | Human Operators | AI Voice Agent | Improvement |
|:-------|:--------------:|:--------------:|:-----------:|
| PTP Rate | 0.14% | **0.29%** | 🟢 **2.07× better** |
| Campaign Cost | $39,500 | **$8,500** | 🟢 **$31,000+ saved** |
| Payments Recovered | ~$0 | **$10,000** | 🟢 Previously uncollectible |
| Campaign Duration | ~2 months | **1 extra day** | ⚡ 60× faster |
 
> **Key Lesson:** Full automation is feasible, but **40–60% is economically optimal** — balancing AI efficiency with human empathy for complex cases.
